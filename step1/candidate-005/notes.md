# Notes - 005

Observations:

- It keeps a record of how many spins have been played and on
  which spin number the player won
- This machine has no sound effect

Bugs:

- The token increment is inconsistent as it increases in
  intervals of [5, 10, 20, 50]

Interesting behavior:

- There is no restart button when the player runs out of tokens,
  but the game will automatically restart after 1-3 seconds
